My Journey

I believe great software starts with someone asking: "What could go wrong?"

I started my career fascinated by the idea that a single overlooked edge case could cascade into a real-world failure. That curiosity led me from functional testing at UBS, where I became the go-to person for payment reconciliation, to building full automation frameworks at Roche and Optum. Along the way, I picked up a dual role as a Business Analyst because understanding what we're building matters just as much as testing how it works. Today, I'm drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of reliability and scale. Automation, for me, isn't just about saving time. It's about protecting people's health records, financial transactions, and trust. I'm looking for a team in Singapore where I can bring this same intensity, grow into performance engineering, and contribute to systems that truly matter.
